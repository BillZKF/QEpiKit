Visual Example of Person to Person Transmission Using Refbook

  • This is a person-to-person Succeptible, Exposed, Infected, Removed model
  • Here Exposed means that an individual has had at least one contact with an Infected individual.
  • Each "contact" between an Infected and Succeptible (or Exposed) individual transfers some pathogen load to that individual. (Think a cough in someones face.)
  • At each step, the prob of response for an Exposed individual is calculated for their pathogen load and compared against a random (min:0,max:1) draw. If the p of response is greater than the draw, the individual is infected.
